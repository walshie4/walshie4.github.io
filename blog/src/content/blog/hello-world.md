Every blog starts with one of these.

I've been thinking about getting a place on the internet that's just mine — no feed,
no algorithm deciding who sees what, no engagement numbers to obsess over.

So here it is. A corner. I'll write when I have something to say.

Topics will probably include: software, building things, cities, whatever I'm currently
reading, and the occasional half-formed idea I need to think through out loud.

No schedule. No newsletter. No comments (for now).

Just writing.
